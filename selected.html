Selected Collaborations and Productions

Selected
Collaborations

Mighty ReArranger by Robert Plant and the Strange Sensation
The second of 4 albums co-written with Robert Plant, this album reached no.4 in the UK Charts

In Trance by Juju
Third album with Gambian Griot Juldeh Camara was Mojo magazine’s No 1 World Music album of the year 2011

Rising Above Bedlam by Jah Wobble’s Invaders Of The Heart
Nominated for Mercury Prize 1992

Notte Della Taranta
Adams and Camara guest on this epic reimagining of Taranta music led by Ludovico Einaudi and Mauro Durante

Les Triaboliques
Trio Recording with ex- Magazine and 3 Mustaphas 3 string virtuoso Ben Mandelson and PiL/Mekons guitarist Lu Edmonds

Selected
Productions

Aman Iman by Tinariwen
Benchmark of Tuareg guitar music

Zoom by Rachid Taha
Algerian maverick’s late masterpiece, featuring cameos from Brian Eno, Mick Jones and Rai Diva Cheba Fadela

Alone by Terakaft
“Rockier, angrier, irresistible“ - Neil Spencer The Guardian

Matchless by Deep Cabaret
Bagpipes and throat singing with Steve Lewis’ idiosyncratic lyrical mash-ups of flawed humanity

Lo’Jo - Transe De Papier
Lo’Jo continue their dream-like oeuvre with the remarkable “Transe De Papier“ - Le Monde.
Released December 2020, with Guest appearances from Tony Allen, Robert Wyatt and Karl Hyde.
