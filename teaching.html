Teaching

I’ve been a listener and a fan all my life and I’ve absorbed techniques and approaches from the incredible musicians I’ve been lucky to work with - as a guitarist I haven’t only learnt from other guitarists, but from master percussionists, who have shown me the infinite secrets of polyrhythm and syncopation, from singers, whose verve and phrasing teach about presence and space, and from countless master musicians scales and grooves speak directly to the heart. I’m happy to work with musicians of any level, to broaden their musical horizons and to help them find the most impactful way of getting their sound across.
